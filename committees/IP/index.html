IP

Students will participate in a simulation involving IP members, acting as unbiased journalists reporting on conference proceedings. They investigate committee debates, conduct interviews, edit articles, and cover press conferences. They observe simulations and disseminate information to all LCIMUN participants. IP members must conduct background research, gather relevant information, and conduct live interviews. Photos taken by IP members will only be used for report submissions and compilation of LCIMUN International materials.
