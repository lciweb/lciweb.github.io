SOCHUM

SOCHUM, or the Social, Humanitarian, and Cultural Affairs Committee, is the United Nations body addressing global social and cultural issues, promoting human rights, and fostering international cooperation for positive change.

UNODC

UNODC, the United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime, strives to enhance global security and development by combating illicit drugs, human trafficking, corruption, and organized crime through global collaboration and capacity-building.

IP

International Press extensively covers UN meetings, reporting on diplomatic discussions, policy decisions, and global issues. They contribute to transparency, public awareness, and accountability, shaping international discourse and cooperation.
