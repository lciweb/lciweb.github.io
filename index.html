Overview

MUN, or Model United Nations is a simulation that mirrors the United Nations, allows students to engage in diplomatic discussions, debate global concerns, and collaborate on impactful resolutions, through which the students gain important traits including public speaking, negotiating and awareness of global issues. Delegates work tirelessly to represent their assigned countries' unique perspectives and create solutions that benefit the international community.

Through this process, students gain an appreciation for diplomacy and learn the value of effective teamwork and cooperation. LCIMUN offers a distinct experience for all participants be it for the IP or the delegations, by focusing on relevant global affairs that needs to be addressed. LCIMUN stands distinct by participating in an MUN challenge proposed globally by UNHCR. LCIMUN is sure to deliver an impeccable experience and a lasting impression for all those who take part.
